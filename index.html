Your AI-Powered Assistant

The interface is designed to feel like a familiar chat app, but with powerful GIS capabilities under the hood.

  • Natural Language Input: Use the text bar at the bottom to type commands like "Calculate area of polygons" or "Create 100m buffer".
  • Quick Suggestions: Not sure what to type? The main screen offers one-click shortcuts for common tasks like NDVI download.
  • Real-time Feedback: See your chat history and the AI's processing status instantly.

Complete Control & Tools

Manage your workflow effectively with our comprehensive tool menu.

  • Dashboard & Settings: Configure your API keys (Google Earth Engine/OpenAI) and view usage stats.
  • Tutorials: Built-in guides to help you master AI-GIS workflows quickly.
  • Export Chat: Save your analysis steps and code snippets for your reports or research papers.
